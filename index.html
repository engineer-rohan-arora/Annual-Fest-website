Welcome To Dharohar 2K19

Dharohar is a students' festival held annually at Quantum University. Dharohar 2019 marks the eleventh edition of this popular event. Over the years Dharohar has provided students a stage to explore and exhibit their creative identities. The event has emerged as a confluence of students for inter-college activities and talent shows. Dharohar is held in Four categories.
